Before You Start

Required Employment Forms

Download and complete the required federal tax form before your first day. Bring a signed copy with you or email it to us with your application.

W-4 — Employee's Withholding Certificate

Required by the IRS for all new employees. Tells us how much federal income tax to withhold from your paycheck. Complete and return this before or on your first day.

Download W-4 (PDF) W-4 Instructions

I-9 — Employment Eligibility Verification

Required by federal law to verify your identity and authorization to work in the United States. You will complete this with your supervisor on your first day. Bring valid ID documents.

Download I-9 (PDF) I-9 Instructions

Completed forms can be emailed to ncosamacorp@gmail.com or brought in person to our Brooklyn office.
